Sub-Marino's is a multi-generation, Italian style deli. Our deli was started in 1934 by Vin Marino, a recently immigrated Italian who wanted to share his passion for cuisine with the local community. All those years later and our establishment is still up and running, serving the community an assortment of hearty subs, along with specialty meals. We offer all these,along with your favorite sides and drinks. Come on in and treat yourself to a delicious sndwhcih.
